THE BAND

THE NEIGHBOURHOOD

A ALTERNATIVE ROCK/POP BAND

They chose the British spelling of "neighbourhood" on the advice of their manager, in order to distinguish themselves from a band that already used the American word "neighborhood". In early 2012, the band released "Female Robbery" and "Sweater Weather". Our band's debut album, I Love You., was released on April 23, 2013 and entered at number thirty-nine on the Billboard 200 chart. Rolling Stone had already released I Love You. on April 16 on their website, describing it as "atmospheric." In April 2014, THENBHD released "Honest", which is part of the soundtrack for The Amazing Spider-Man 2: Rise of Electro (2014). We then released a mixtape titled #000000 & #FFFFFF (the HTML color codes for black and white) in November 2014. That same year, they announced via twitter that "Sweater Weather" was certified platinum in the US.

Jesse James Rutherford (Newbury Park, California, United States, August 21, 1991) is an American singer, rapper, and actor, known for being the vocalist and leader of the band The Neighborhood. At the beginning of 2011, Jesse released "Truth hurts, truth heals", his first solo mixtape, where he demonstrates his skills as a rapper, as he did simultaneously with The Good Boys, the duo he was part of with Jez Dior. In August of that same year, together with guitarists Jeremy Freedman and Zach Abels, drummer Bryan Sammis and bassist Mikey Margott, he formed The Neighborhood.
